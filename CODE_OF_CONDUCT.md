# Code Of Conduct

ScenarioLens welcomes constructive, respectful collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and specific in feedback.
- Assume good intent while being clear about technical risks.
- Keep discussions focused on reproducibility, public-safe data handling, and
  useful autonomy evaluation.
- Respect dataset licenses, access controls, and privacy boundaries.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private data, credentials, or gated dataset contents.
- Misrepresenting ScenarioLens as a production AV system or internal Waymo tool.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that violate
this code. Serious or repeated violations may lead to blocking from the project.
